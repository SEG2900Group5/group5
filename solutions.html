Solutions

With solutions ranging from a digital outfit coordinator to a home security management system, Electra aims at becoming the Number 1 stop to manage one's home.

Universal app

Using our app on your smartphone, you can turn on the lights, play your favourite football match on the TV and even change the colour of the light to match that of your team. The only thing left you do would be to grab popcorn!

Home Security

With our new smart home solution, you can now manage your security system right from your smartphone. You can analyse live videos, get text alerts when breaches are detected, lock your door if you left it unlocked, only to mention these. Additional features also allow you to check whether you left the stove on when leaving home for your peace on mind.

Digital Outfit Coordinator

Have you ever spent hours deciding on which outfit to wear? If yes, you will surely appreciate our outfit coordinator. Based on your own style and weather prevailing, our program will choose an attire for you. No longer need to waste countless hours picking clothes!

Pet Care

Apart from watching live videos of your pets at home when you are away, you can also use our smart pet care solution to feed your pets, give them water automatically, all to make sure your pets are safe at home.
